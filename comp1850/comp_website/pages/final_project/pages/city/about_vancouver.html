About Vancouver

Vancouver and Region

Vancouver City

Vancouver is the biggest city in Britsh Columbia (BC) province. Although many people think that Vancouver is also the capital, this is not true. The capital of Britsh Columbia is Victoria, a smaller city in the Vancouver Island. The region in which Vancouver is located is known as "Lower Mainland", where about 60% of BC population lives. The is membership of a metropolis named "Metro Vancouver". It is composed of 23 members being:

  • 13 Cities;
  • 4 District municipality;
  • 3 Villages;
  • 1 Island municipality;
  • 1 Indian reserve (Treaty First Nation);
  • 1 Regional district electoral area.
Vancouver Maps

Vancouver is at Canadia west coast and has important rivers and inlets flowing through it. Because of this, the port business is very strong in this area (and one of most important in Canada), as well as the foresting business. Vancouver is also well-known by been a green city, both for having many trees and parks as for its policies regarding sustainability.

Vancouverites

Vancouver Ethnicity

The city of Vancouver has about 600 thousand people while the metropolitan region has up to 2.4 million residents. These numbers make Metro Vancouver one of most density regions in Canada, with Vancouver having 5,249 people per square km.

The ethnic of the city is quite mixed whit about half of people having descendency or being immigrants. Because of this, Vancouver is a multicultural city, where many languages are spoken, including in government services such as 911. The largest immigrant group in the city are the Chinese.

Weather: Too much cold, too much hot?

Vancouver Weather

Not that cool and not that hot. Vancouver is one of the most moderated cities in Canada regarding to weather. Of course, during the winter it will have some cold days, with temperatures under zero Celsius degree and snow. But comparing to other major cities in Canada, the average temperature either in winter and summer are moderated. What you really need to care about Vancouver is the raining. Not wonder, Vancouver is also known as "Raincouver".

But again, is not that bad. Vancouver "just" occupy the 9th position in the "Most raining cities in Canada". Anyway, it is better to be prepared to use some umbrellas. And remember, Vancouver is in Canada: When it said that here is not that cold it is compared with other places in Canada. It does not mean that looks like a tropical beach.
