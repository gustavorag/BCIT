  1. Project Theme

    The website will have as Theme "Studying and Living in Vancouver". There, people who want to move to Vancouver will find information and tips for move forward in this goal.

  2. Audience

    The site will aim for internaltion students who are thinkig in come to Vancouver to study or are alerady here and need some information or tips about the city of Vancouver. Therefor, the audience is basically:

    • International students who are outside Canada
    • International students who recently arrived on Canada

  3. Pages' Description

    • Living

      This page will aboard many aspects of living in Vancouver such as living costs, housing, where to do grocery, description of neighborhoods, etc.

    • Studying

      Here the user will find some information about colleges, permits, costs, etc.

    • Trasnport

      This section will be reserved to inform about how the public transit works, costs, maps, etc.

    • Entretainment

      If the student wants to enjoy the city, he/she will find some nice tips about what to enjoy in the city.

    • Contact

      This page will yield ways to contact the page's author.

  4. Objectives

    Some objectives of this site are:

    • To help international students
    • To yield information about Vancouver City
    • To provide external resources about Vancouver City
